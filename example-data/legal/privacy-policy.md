# SpiritVale Privacy Policy

**Version 1.1**  
**Last Updated:** June 16th, 2026

## 1. Quick Overview

SpiritVale and its team care about your privacy. This Policy explains what information we collect, why we use it, how we may share it, and what rights you may have.

This Policy covers the SpiritVale Demo, the SpiritVale Live version, SpiritVale.info, the wiki and database hosted on SpiritVale.info, the official Discord server, Steam, Ko-fi, Kickstarter, and other official SpiritVale services where applicable.

The SpiritVale Playtest is treated as a legacy version of the service. Although the SpiritVale Playtest may no longer be publicly available, records from the SpiritVale Playtest may still be handled under this Policy where needed for support, moderation, safety, accounting, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or service history.

## 4. Information We Collect

We collect different types of information depending on how you use SpiritVale, its official services, and related third-party services.

### Account and Platform Data

We may collect account and platform data to run the game and manage our services. This may include your Steam ID, character names, Device UID, IP address, Discord username and tag, and account information if you create a SpiritVale.info, wiki, or database account.

Steam ID, Device UID, and IP address are needed to provide access to the game and support our service, moderation, and support systems. Device UID is provided through Steam. Other account and platform data may be used for moderation and service management.

### Game Data

We collect game data to run the game and keep track of player records. This may include character levels, items, gold balance, location, login information, inventory, account progress, cloud save data, and other game records.

We also collect information about player activity involving other players. This includes trading, vending, buying, selling, and other player-to-player interactions.

We may publicly display certain game records on SpiritVale.info, including leaderboards, vending status, game statistics, and other public database features.

Game data may relate to the SpiritVale Demo, the SpiritVale Live version, or legacy SpiritVale Playtest records where those records are still needed.

### SpiritVale.info, Wiki, and Database Data

We may collect data from SpiritVale.info and its related services, including the website, wiki, database, leaderboards, vending tools, public pages, and other related features.

If you create an account, this may include your username, password information, and IP address. We also keep edit history and may log IP addresses connected to website, wiki, or database activity.

Account passwords are stored securely in protected form and are separate from SpiritVale’s main game authentication system at this time.

SpiritVale.info, the wiki, and the database are public services. Some information shared, submitted, edited, or created there may be visible to other users by design.

If you log in to SpiritVale.info, the wiki, or database, essential login or session cookies may be used to keep the service working properly.

### Technical Data

We collect only limited technical data. This includes IP address and Device UID. We do not currently collect crash logs, error reports, performance data, device type, operating system, or browser type unless those details are provided by you through support, bug reports, platform tools, or other official services.

The technical data we collect is gathered automatically when needed to run the service, support moderation, and protect our systems.

### Community and Support Data

We may collect community and support records when you contact us or take part in our community spaces. This includes Discord moderator tickets, messages sent to staff or moderators, bug reports, support requests, user reports, appeals, moderation notes, and enforcement history.

These records are kept private and are only available to approved users who need access for support, moderation, safety, or service management.

### Payment, Crowdfunding, and Supporter Data

If you support SpiritVale through Ko-fi, Kickstarter, or related payment services, we may receive supporter, backer, pledge, and transaction information made available to us through those services.

This may include your name or username, email address, donation, membership, purchase, pledge, reward, survey response, or transaction details.

For purchases, memberships, pledges, rewards, or other supporter benefits that require fulfillment or tax handling, we may also receive mailing address, phone number, or other shipping-related details where needed.

We may use this information to manage donations, memberships, purchases, pledges, rewards, supporter benefits, support requests, accounting, taxes, fraud prevention, disputes, chargebacks, and fulfillment.

## 5. How We Collect Information

We collect information in a few different ways, depending on how our services and related third-party services are used.

### Information You Give Us Directly

Some information is provided by you.

- When you create or edit a SpiritVale.info, wiki, or database account, you provide your username and password information.
- When you contact support, submit an appeal, or open a moderator ticket, you may also provide information such as your in-game character name, preferred server, the name of another player, your Steam ID if needed, and the reason for your request.
- When you submit a bug report, you may choose to provide similar information. Bug report details are optional.
- If you post, submit, or edit content on SpiritVale.info, the wiki, or database, we may also receive your username and IP address, along with the content you submit.
- If you respond to Kickstarter surveys, supporter forms, fulfillment requests, or similar requests, you may provide information needed to manage rewards, fulfillment, support, or tax handling.

### Information Collected Automatically

Some information is collected automatically by the system.

- When you launch or use the game, we may automatically collect your Steam ID, IP address, Device UID, login times, session data, and account activity.
- When you visit SpiritVale.info, the wiki, or database, we may automatically collect your IP address. If you log in to these services, essential login or session cookies may also be used.
- Some public game records, such as vending status, leaderboard data, game statistics, or other public database records, may also be updated automatically through normal use of the service.

### Information Received from Other Platforms

Some information is received from the third-party services we use.

- From Steam, we may receive your Steam ID, IP address, and Device UID.
- From Discord, we may receive your Discord username, display name, and server tag.
- From Ko-fi, we may receive supporter and transaction information such as your name or username, email address, donation, membership, or purchase details, transaction records, and shipping-related details where needed for fulfillment or tax handling.
- From Kickstarter, we may receive backer, pledge, reward, survey, fulfillment, and transaction information made available to creators, such as your name or username, email address, pledge details, reward selection, survey responses, shipping details, and related records where needed.

### Information Created by SpiritVale

Some information is created by SpiritVale while running the game, operating SpiritVale.info, managing the wiki or database, or interacting with the community.

This may include support tickets, moderation tracking, reports, appeals, enforcement records, fraud records, anti-cheat records, and service history connected to the SpiritVale Demo, SpiritVale Live version, or legacy SpiritVale Playtest.

For safety reasons, we do not publicly describe all internal review or enforcement methods in detail. These records are kept private and are limited to members of the SpiritVale team who need access for support, moderation, safety, or service management.

## 17. Children’s Privacy

SpiritVale uses Steam for account authentication and follows Steam’s account age rules.

The minimum age to create a Steam account is 13. In some countries, Steam may require a higher age or parental consent. SpiritVale is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under that age outside of Steam’s account system.

Players may use SpiritVale if they are using a Steam account that meets Steam’s rules, including any parental consent rules that apply.

If we learn that a child has used the service in a way that does not meet these rules, we may review the account and take appropriate action.

Questions or concerns about children’s privacy may be sent to [spiritvaledev@gmail.com](mailto:spiritvaledev@gmail.com).
